All In One Destination For Arecanut Trees
The areca nut is the fruit of the areca palm which grows in much of the tropical Pacific, South Asia and Southeast Asia. It is commonly referred to as betel nut, and is often used for chewing. However, there are quite a number of uses of this nut. In India, Karnataka is the largest producer of Arecanut.
Our farmers often end up with questions during the entire lifecycle of this crop, and this website aims at answering those queries by being a single destination for all those questions.
